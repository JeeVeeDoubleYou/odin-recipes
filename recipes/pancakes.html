Pancakes

Description

Big fluffy pancakes! The recipe comes straight from an old recipe's book, owned by "dakota kelly's" grandmother. The total time it takes to make is 20 minutes and the quatities are enough for eight serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 of a teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
