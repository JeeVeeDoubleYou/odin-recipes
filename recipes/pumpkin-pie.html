Pumpkin Pie

Description

Yummy Pumkin pie :) Perfect for a Thanksgiving feast, or any other occasion! Cooking time is 55 minutes, with a total time that adds up to 1 hour and 10 minutes. The quantities are enough for 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 15 once can pumpkin
  • 14 ounce condensed milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 9 inch unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into crust. Bake 15 minutes.
  2. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean. Cool. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
